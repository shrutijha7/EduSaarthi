Q1: The principles discussed in the course emphasize performance and cost analysis, instruction set design, pipelining, memory hierarchy, and __________ management.
Answer: virtual memory

Q2: A key objective of the course is to familiarize students with the basic components and design of the CPU, including the control unit and the __________.
Answer: ALU

Q3: The fundamental structure of computers covers functional units, basic operational concepts, and the principles of the __________ Architecture.
Answer: Von-Neumann

Q4: Computer architectures are often classified based on their instruction sets, such as Reduced Instruction Set Computer (RISC) and __________.
Answer: CISC

Q5: In the study of multiprocessors, critical topics include Inter Processor Arbitration, Inter processor Communication and Synchronization, and __________.
Answer: Cache Coherence
